Festival Poster Campaign

Poster Design / Visual Branding

This project is a two-poster campaign designed to promote a music festival using a consistent visual system. The goal was to create bold, attention-grabbing layouts that still feel cohesive across the series. I focused on strong typography, hierarchy, and a clear brand direction so the posters could work both online and in print.

Role: Designer Tools: Adobe (PS/AI) Deliverables: 2 Posters
